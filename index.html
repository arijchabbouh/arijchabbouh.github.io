About

I build things.
I also care how they look.

I'm Arij — a Computer Engineering student at ENICarthage, coming out of two years of Classes Préparatoires at IPEI Nabeul. CPGE taught me to work under pressure and think rigorously. ENICarthage taught me to ship.

My technical home is the full stack: React on the frontend, Java + Spring Boot on the backend, Oracle and REST APIs in the middle. I built my first real production app at Aftercode — a complete web platform called BeTech, from wireframe to deployment.

I'm drawn to problems where the technical and the visual intersect — which is probably why I ended up doing UI/UX work alongside backend development, and why I picked up Figma before most of my classmates picked up Docker.

Currently exploring machine learning — my academic project included a TF-IDF cosine similarity engine, and I presented a virtual try-on AI platform at TSYP 12. That's the direction I'm heading.
